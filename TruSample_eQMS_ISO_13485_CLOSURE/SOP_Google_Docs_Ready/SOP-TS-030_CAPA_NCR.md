SOP-TS-030 — CAPA & NCR Procedure
Purpose
Define how nonconformities are recorded, investigated, and closed via CAPA processes to ensure corrective and preventive actions are effective.

Scope
All product, process, or system nonconformities discovered internally or via customer feedback.

Procedure
1. Detection & Recording
   - Record NCR in CAPA/NCR log with unique ID, date, reporter, description, severity.
2. Containment
   - Implement immediate containment actions where needed; document evidence.
3. Root Cause Analysis
   - Perform RCA (5 Whys / Fishbone); document findings and proposed corrective actions.
4. Action Plan & Implementation
   - Assign actions with owners and due dates; record in Mgmt Actions if needed.
5. Verification & Effectiveness
   - Verify action implementation; collect metrics showing effectiveness.
6. Closure
   - Close NCR when verification shows effectiveness; record closure evidence and approver.

Records
- CAPA/NCR log entries
- FRM-TS-204 for linked Mgmt Actions
- Evidence attachments in Documents_Index
