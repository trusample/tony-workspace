SOP-TS-040 — Training & Competency
Purpose
Define required training, delivery methods, and recordkeeping to ensure personnel competence.

Scope
All personnel performing work that affects product quality or QMS operations.

Procedure
1. Training Matrix
   - Define roles and required training modules; store in Training Matrix sheet.
2. Training Delivery
   - Provide training via live sessions, documentation, or on-the-job training.
3. Recording Completion
   - Record training completion date, trainer, and evidence (certificate or signed form) in Training Matrix.
4. Recurrent Training
   - Schedule refresher training per SOP requirements or regulatory needs.

Records
- Training Matrix (sheet)
- Training evidence files (stored and linked in Documents_Index)
