SOP-TS-050 — Records Retention & Access Control
Purpose
Define where records are stored, retention periods, backup strategy, and access controls for the eQMS.

Scope
All QMS records generated by personnel and automation.

Procedure
1. Storage locations
   - Primary: Google Drive under /TruSample/eQMS with subfolders (SOPs, Forms, Records, Batch_Records, COA)
   - Archive: /TruSample/eQMS/Archive for obsolete documents
2. Retention periods
   - Define retention per record type (e.g., Batch records: 10 years; COA: 10 years; Training records: 5 years)
3. Backup & Recovery
   - Backup policy: daily snapshots to backup location and periodic exports
4. Access control
   - Assign folder-level permissions; maintain list of account owners in CONFIG
5. Disposal
   - Securely remove records after retention period; update Reference_Map and Documents_Index

Records
- Documents_Index entries with retention metadata
- Access control list in CONFIG or admin sheet
