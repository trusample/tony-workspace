SOP-TS-080 — Design Control & RUO Product Governance (Synthetic Male AB Serum)
Purpose
Define design control activities, risk management, and RUO governance for product development and claims.

Scope
Covers design inputs/outputs, DHF artifacts, risk assessments, and claims justification for RUO product(s).

Procedure
1. Define Design Inputs
   - Capture functional, safety, and regulatory constraints; link to eQMS Master Steps.
2. Design Outputs & DHF
   - Record design outputs (formulas, specs, process parameters) and store in Products/Synthetic_Male_AB_Serum folder.
3. Risk Management
   - Perform hazard analysis/FMEA; record mitigations and acceptability criteria.
4. Verification & Validation
   - Define V&V activities, acceptance criteria, and record results (attach to Documents_Index).
5. Claims & RUO governance
   - Document RUO scope and claims (recombinant-only, sterile-filtered), including limitations and labeling requirements.

Records
- Design history documents in Products/Synthetic_Male_AB_Serum
- Risk assessment artifacts and V&V results
- Documents_Index entries linking DHF artifacts
