## 1) What we’re selling (one-sentence)
**TruSample ExtractLess™** enables **nucleic-acid extraction-free** processing for RT-qPCR/PCR by **binding inhibitors**, **lysing cells**, and **stabilizing nucleic acids** so labs can skip extraction and reduce time/complexity.

## 2) Key claims (from manuals/protocol)
Use these as message hooks and qualification questions.

### A) Workflow simplicity (extraction-free)
- “Extraction-free sample processing **without centrifugations or other sample manipulations**.”
- Mix **1:1** sample with Reagent (example: **20 µL + 20 µL**), then heat.

### B) Speed (organism-dependent)
- Mammalian: **~5 min**
- Viruses: **~10–15 min**
- Bacteria/Fungi: **~15–20 min**

### C) Storage / logistics
- Shipped and stored at **room temperature** (recommended **4°C–25°C**).

### D) Sample guidance (protocol)
- Protocol explicitly calls out:
  - transport media / swab samples in **non-inactivating** medium
  - RBC-containing samples requiring extra care
  - debris removal options (settle/low-spin/filter)
  - optional wash/pellet steps for high inhibitors / low concentration targets

### E) Compatibility caveats
- Not compatible with media containing **guanidinium thiocyanate**, **alcohols**, or other strong enzyme inhibitors (user must confirm compatibility).
